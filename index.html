Setup Guide: How to connect your Google Sheet

No extra publishing required: You can now paste your standard edit URL! Just make sure your Sheet is viewable by anyone with the link.

To connect your sprint tracking spreadsheet, configure your Google Sheet sharing options:

  1. Open your spreadsheet in Google Sheets.
  2. Click the blue Share button in the top right corner.
  3. Under General Access, change the permission from Restricted to Anyone with the link can view.
  4. Copy the sharing URL from your address bar or the share modal dialog and paste it in the configuration input box above.
